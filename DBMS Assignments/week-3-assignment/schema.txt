Database Schema Design
Movies Collection

Fields: title, release_year, genre, director_id, actors (array of actor IDs)
Each movie document will reference its director and list of actors.
Directors Collection

Fields: name, nationality, movies_directed (array of movie IDs)
Each director document will have a list of movies they directed.
Actors Collection

Fields: name, nationality, movies_acted_in (array of movie IDs)
Each actor document will have a list of movies they acted in.
